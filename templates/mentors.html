Caitlyn Shum

Mechanical Engineering

Caitlyn Shum discovered her interest in sustainable building technologies during her undergraduate studies in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Alberta. That passion led her to pursue a Masters of Science. Her research regarding climate change resiliency in building explored residential and commercial building retrofit strategies for wildfire smoke mitigation and energy savings. Today, Caitlyn applies her expertise as a Mechanical Buildings Consultant at CIMA+ where she designs mechanical systems for commercial and institutional buildings.
