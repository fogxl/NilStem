About

What is NIL-STEM?

NIL-STEM is a virtual, non-monetary program designed to help high school students, especially those in rural areas, explore STEM programs offered by top universities across Canada and discover future opportunities in science, technology, and engineering fields.

Goals of NIL-STEM

Provide students with opportunities to:

Broaden awareness of diverse pursuable fields in post-secondary

Help inspire interest in post-secondary studies

Network and learn from successful professionals in the STEM fields

Learn about academic programs and university life directly from students pursuing studies
